# Project Deliverables — Dozen Hotel Supplies AI System

**Status:** APPROVED
**Prepared:** 2026-05-07

## What We Are Building

A two-phase system for Dozen Hotel Supplies. Phase 1 delivers a brand-new website and an AI-powered email outreach system that activates your existing 522-prospect pipeline and finds new leads. Phase 2 adds WhatsApp and live website chat so the AI can continue conversations on the channels your clients already use.

---

## Deliverables

### Phase 1 — Website

- [ ] A brand-new Dozen website built from scratch, replacing the current one
- [ ] A company story and "About Us" section introducing Dozen, your Egyptian supply heritage, and your presence in Zanzibar
- [ ] A full product catalog covering all current lines: towels, bed linen, bedding, F&B linen, bathrobes, slippers, and kitchen/sanitation — with sizes, colors, weights, and photos
- [ ] A "Trusted by Elites" client showcase displaying all 30+ hotel logos
- [ ] A request-a-quote flow where any visitor can describe what they need and receive a tailored response from the Dozen AI assistant
- [ ] Mobile-friendly design that looks and works well on phones (how most of your clients browse)

### Phase 1 — AI Outreach & Lead Activation

- [ ] An AI assistant that reads your existing 522-prospect spreadsheet and reaches out to each contact individually by email, introducing Dozen and their products
- [ ] Personalized outreach that references the prospect's hotel type, location, and likely product needs — not a generic mass email
- [ ] Automatic follow-up emails for prospects who don't respond the first time
- [ ] A continuously updated lead tracker showing which prospects have been contacted, who responded, who is in conversation, and who placed an inquiry
- [ ] New prospect discovery: the AI searches Google Maps and the web to find hotels, resorts, and hospitality businesses in Zanzibar not already in your list, and adds qualified ones to the pipeline

### Phase 1 — AI Sales Assistant (Email)

- [ ] The AI handles all inbound email inquiries from prospects — answering product questions, explaining options, and suggesting the right products for their property type
- [ ] When a prospect asks for a price, the AI provides indicative pricing and immediately notifies you (owner) and your sales manager via WhatsApp so you can review and approve a formal quote
- [ ] An auto-response holds the conversation warmly while the quote is being approved, so the prospect never feels ignored
- [ ] All conversations are logged and accessible to your team in one place

### Phase 2 — WhatsApp Business

- [ ] A Dozen WhatsApp Business account fully set up and connected to the AI assistant
- [ ] Prospects who have shown interest (replied to email, submitted a quote request, or contacted Dozen directly) can continue their conversation on WhatsApp
- [ ] The AI handles WhatsApp conversations the same way it handles email — product questions, pricing guidance, quote routing to you for approval
- [ ] Your team can jump into any WhatsApp conversation at any time to take over from the AI

### Phase 2 — Live Chat on the Website

- [ ] A chat widget on the Dozen website so any visitor can talk to the AI assistant in real time
- [ ] The AI introduces Dozen, helps visitors find the right products, and routes serious inquiries to the quote approval flow

### Phase 2 — Expanded Prospecting

- [ ] Additional lead discovery using LinkedIn and professional directories to find procurement managers and hotel owners not reachable via Google Maps or web search
- [ ] All new prospects added to the same lead tracker used in Phase 1

---

### Not Included

- Physical order fulfilment, stock management, or inventory tracking
- Payment processing or invoicing
- A customer login portal or account management area
- Any products not currently in the Dozen catalog (expanding the catalog is a separate project)
- Advertising or paid media campaigns
- Any market outside Zanzibar and Tanzania in Phase 1 (expansion can be discussed for later phases)
