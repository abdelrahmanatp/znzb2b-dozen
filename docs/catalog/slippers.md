## Size Guide
**Slippers:** One size fits all (OSFA)  
- Typical range: sizes 5–11 (women's EU 35–43, men's EU 38–46)
- Adjustable or stretchy design accommodates variation
- Some styles feature elastic ankle strap for secure fit

**Recommendation:** Order by pairs; keep 10–15% extra inventory for replacement due to normal wear and loss

## Ordering Recommendations

**Budget properties (guesthouses, villas):** Terry cotton slippers (white) — cost-effective, durable, comfortable  
**Mid-range hotels:** Terry (white) + waffle (white) mix for seasonal preferences; colored terry for visual variety  
**Luxury resorts:** Velour (white or custom colors) with logo embroidery; supplement with waffle for spa use  
**Spa facilities:** Premium velour primary offering; waffle backups for quick drying during peak use

## Care & Maintenance
- Machine washable (100% cotton; no special care required)
- Gentle cycle recommended to preserve embroidery
- Tumble dry on low to preserve elasticity and shape
- Regular rotation extends lifespan to 2–3 years with proper care
