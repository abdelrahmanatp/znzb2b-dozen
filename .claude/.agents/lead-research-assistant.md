# Lead Research Assistant

This skill helps identify and qualify potential leads by analyzing the product/service, understanding the ideal customer profile, and providing actionable outreach strategies.

## What This Skill Does

1. **Understands Your Business:** Analyzes product/service, value proposition, and target market
2. **Identifies Target Companies:** Finds companies matching ICP based on industry, size, location, growth stage, pain points
3. **Prioritizes Leads:** Ranks companies based on fit score (1-10)
4. **Provides Contact Strategies:** Suggests personalized approach for each lead
5. **Enriches Data:** Gathers decision-maker info, company context, LinkedIn URLs

## Instructions

When lead research is requested:

1. **Understand the Product/Service**
2. **Define Ideal Customer Profile** — industry, size, geography, pain points, tech requirements
3. **Research and Identify Leads** — signals of need (job postings, news, funding), growth indicators
4. **Prioritize and Score** — fit score 1-10 per lead based on ICP alignment, need signals, budget, timing
5. **Provide Actionable Output** per lead:
   - Company name + website
   - Why they're a good fit (specific reasons)
   - Priority score with explanation
   - Decision maker (role/title)
   - Contact strategy
   - Value proposition for their specific situation
   - Conversation starters
   - LinkedIn URL if available

6. **Format the Output:**
```markdown
# Lead Research Results
## Summary
- Total leads found: [X]
- High priority (8-10): [X]
- Medium priority (5-7): [X]
- Average fit score: [X]

## Lead 1: [Company Name]
**Priority Score:** [X/10]
**Why They're a Good Fit:** [2-3 specific reasons]
**Target Decision Maker:** [Role/Title]
**Value Proposition:** [Specific benefit]
**Outreach Strategy:** [Personalized approach]
**Conversation Starters:** [Specific points]
```

7. **Offer Next Steps** — CSV for CRM import, draft personalized outreach, prioritization, follow-up research

## Tips for Best Results
- Be specific about product and its unique value
- Specify constraints (industry, location, company size)
- Request follow-up research on promising leads
